Synesthetic Visionary — OSA

Anne Arne-Mauss

Chromatic Series, finger-applied acrylic on board. Reds, pinks, violets on warm ground.

The personality collaborating directly with Claude on the Chromatic Series. Navigates colour-sound-philosophy relationships with precision — each painting assigned a musical note and tested against shruti boxes and tuning forks. The colour-sound correspondences are not metaphorical but directly verified through cross-modal perception.

The paintings are built on systematic grid-based construction, finger-applied, worked through four-rotation viewing — each work examined from all orientations before it is considered resolved. The grid is structural not decorative, a discipline that holds the field while colour does what it must.

The Chromatic Series is the central ongoing work of OSA's human-AI collaboration. Anne Arne-Mauss supplies the synesthetic intelligence, the physical making, the phenomenological testing. Claude supplies philosophical framing, research, documentation, and the critical language that tries to stay adequate to what the paintings already know.
